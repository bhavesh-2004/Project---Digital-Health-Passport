Data Privacy and Security

1. Privacy Policy

Digital Health Passport is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. Our Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and disclose your information. By using our platform, you agree to the terms outlined in our Privacy Policy.

Read our Privacy Policy

2. Data Collection

We collect various types of information from our users, including personal and health-related data. This information is used to provide our services, improve user experience, and ensure the security of our platform. We only collect data that is necessary for the operation of our services.

3. Data Security

We take data security seriously and implement industry-standard measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. Our platform uses encryption, access controls, and other security measures to safeguard your data.

4. User Controls

We provide users with controls and options to manage their data privacy preferences. You can review, update, or delete your information through our platform settings. We also offer options for data export and account deletion.

5. Compliance

We comply with applicable data protection laws and regulations, including GDPR and HIPAA, where applicable. Our commitment to data privacy and security extends to our third-party service providers and partners.

6. Data Breach Response

In the event of a data breach, we have procedures in place to promptly assess the situation, notify affected users, and take appropriate remedial actions. We prioritize transparency and communication with our users in such situations.
