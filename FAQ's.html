Frequently Asked Questions

A digital health passport is a digital platform or application that securely stores and manages an individual's health information, particularly related to COVID-19 testing, vaccination status, and other relevant medical data. These passports are designed to facilitate safe travel and access to various services by providing proof of a person's health status.

DHPA number is a 14 digit number that will uniquely identify you as a participant in India's digital healthcare ecosystem. DHPA number will establish a strong and trustable identity for you that will be accepted by healthcare providers.

The DHPA number is often used in conjunction with robust privacy and security measures to protect individuals' personal health information. Encryption, authentication protocols, and data access controls help ensure that sensitive data remains secure and confidential.
